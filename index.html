Welcome to Printronix Computing Solution

Printronix Computing Solution is a professional computer and printer hardware company based in Kasthamandap, Kathmandu. We supply high-quality IT products and printer solutions to businesses, offices, and retailers.

Our main strength is importing printer cartridges and spare parts from China, ensuring reliable products at competitive prices.
